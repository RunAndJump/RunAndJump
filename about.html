About us!

The inception of runandJUMP came about during an intense round of Super Mario Brothers Wii U. The concept is simple: Just run and jump before a member of your own party tries to throw you into Piranha plant. It quickly evolved into a two-person team creating limited edition, quality enamel pins for not only those embarking on their journey to saving the princess, but for those completing their pokedex, becoming Hokage, and more! Based in Orlando, we are just two anime, Nintendo, and Studio Ghibli enthusiasts with a passion for representing the things we love. We hope you enjoy our dorky heart-work!
Steven and Nicole

runandJump! creators
